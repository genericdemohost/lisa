    The Black Forum of CO-OP City is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing emergency food support to our 55K-person community. The Black Forum’s mission is to provide food pantry services to assist individuals, families, the elderly, disabled and others from the surrounding community, who are facing economic hardships.

    We serve to educate the community on health and wellness awareness issues through motivational speakers, healthy eating seminars, menu planning with registered dieticians, and aerobics classes three times a week with a certified instructor.

    In addition, our organization strives to support our community’s youth leadership development through the arts, positive interactions and an emphasis on academic excellence through our afterschool tutoring sessions for children in 3rd to 6th grade and our track team (the Lion’s Mane) for 6 year olds and up. We are also dedicated to helping our senior citizen population maintain strength and vitality through sponsored outdoor recreation and resort outings.

    Emergency Food Program

    We provide food support to 10475 community members. Patrons outside of CO-OP City are welcome to visit us on a one time basis, where we will also provide referral information to agencies in their local zip code. As a fIrst time participant, please have a current photo ID, such as non-driver or driver’s license, or an ID NYC card. Distribution is Tuesday-Friday, 11AM - 12:45 PM.

    Cooking Class

    If you enjoy cooking or would like to learn how to prepare healthy meals for yourself and your family, please contact us at (718) 543-0000. Classes begin in February 2016, are free to the general public and are part of a partnership with Just Say Yes to Fruits and Vegetables (JSY).

    Defensive Driving

    Black Forum defensive driving course returns in Winter 2016. It’s held at Dreiser Community Center, 177 Dreiser Loop, Room 1 from 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM. Participants have the opportunity to lower their liability insurance costs by 10% and remove up to 4 points from their driver’s license.
